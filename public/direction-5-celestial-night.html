Our Principles

The constellations we follow

Fitrah First

Every soul was born oriented toward the heavens. Our journeys recalibrate that celestial compass — the fitrah — helping you find your true north again.

Presence Over Performance

Under the night sky, no one checks their phone. We create spaces where the only light that matters is starlight and the light within.

The Earth as Teacher

From the orbit of planets to the path of a river, the cosmos is a classroom. We walk the earth and gaze at the sky as eternal students.
